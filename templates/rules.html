USSSA Pitching Rules (11U/12U)

Max Pitches Per Day: 85
Required Rest

Rest days are required based on the number of pitches thrown in a single day.

Pitches Thrown in a Day Required Days of Rest
1 - 200
21 - 351
36 - 502
51 - 653
66+4

Finish the Batter Rule: A pitcher may finish the current batter if the pitch limit is reached during that at-bat. The pitcher must be removed after the at-bat is completed.
